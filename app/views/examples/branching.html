Branching

You may want to show a user different pages, depending on how they've answered a question.

You can make this happen by including value="redirect(<URL>)" in your form inputs. For example:

<input type="radio" name="work-status" value="redirect(branch/employed)"> <input type="radio" name="work-status" value="redirect(branch/selfemployed)"> <input type="radio" name="work-status" value="redirect(branch/director)">

What is your employment status?

What is your employment status?

 

If you need more comple logic to determine which page to show next you can write server-side code to achieve this as in the following example:

/app/routes.js /app/views/examples/branching.html
